    Frequently Asked Questions

    How far in advance should I book?

    We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance, especially during peak season (September-November and March-May). However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings if guides are available. Contact us to check availability for your preferred dates.

    What's included in the tour price?

    All tours include an experienced local guide, entrance fees, and transportation from Sapa town. Multi-day treks include accommodation, all meals, and camping equipment if needed. Specific inclusions vary by tour—check individual tour pages or ask us for details.

    What fitness level do I need?

    We offer treks for all fitness levels. Day treks like Cat Cat Village are suitable for most people with basic fitness. Multi-day treks and the Fansipan summit require good fitness and some hiking experience. Contact us to discuss which trek matches your abilities.

    What should I bring?

    Essential items include comfortable hiking shoes, sun protection, water bottle, rain jacket, and camera. We'll provide a detailed packing list when you book. For multi-day treks, we can arrange porter services if you prefer to travel light.

    Can you customize tours?

    Absolutely! We're happy to customize itineraries based on your interests, fitness level, time constraints, and special requests. Whether you want to focus on photography, cultural immersion, or specific villages, we can create a personalized experience.

    What's your cancellation policy?

    We offer flexible cancellation for weather-related issues. For other cancellations, we provide full refunds if cancelled more than 48 hours before the trek. Contact us for our complete cancellation and refund policy.
