    Our Story

    Pang Trekking was born from a simple passion: sharing the extraordinary beauty and rich culture of Sapa with travelers from around the world. Founded over a decade ago by local guides who grew up in these mountains, we've built our reputation on authentic experiences, deep local knowledge, and genuine hospitality that reflects the spirit of northern Vietnam.

    Our founder, Pang, spent his childhood exploring every trail, valley, and peak in the Hoang Lien Son mountain range. What started as guiding a few backpackers through rice terraces has grown into a respected trekking company that employs dozens of local guides and supports multiple village communities. Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our core mission: creating meaningful connections between travelers and the remarkable landscape and people of Sapa.

    Every member of our team is from the Sapa region, bringing firsthand knowledge of trails, weather patterns, cultural traditions, and hidden gems that only locals know. This authenticity is what sets us apart—when you trek with us, you're not just following a guide, you're walking with someone who calls these mountains home and genuinely loves sharing them with visitors.

    Our Values & Approach

    Authentic Experiences

    We believe in real cultural exchange, not staged performances. Our treks take you to genuine villages where people live traditional lifestyles, and our guides facilitate meaningful interactions that respect local customs and privacy. You'll experience Sapa as it truly is, not a tourist version.

    Sustainable Tourism

    We're committed to tourism that benefits local communities and preserves the natural environment. We employ local guides, source food from village families, and contribute to community development projects. Our small group sizes minimize environmental impact, and we practice Leave No Trace principles on all treks.

    Community Partnership

    A portion of every booking supports local schools, healthcare initiatives, and cultural preservation projects. We work directly with village leaders to ensure tourism benefits the communities we visit. Many of our homestay hosts have been partners for years, and their families have become part of the Pang Trekking extended family.

    Safety & Quality

    Your safety and comfort are paramount. All our guides are trained in first aid and mountain safety, we monitor weather conditions carefully, and we maintain comprehensive insurance coverage. We invest in quality equipment, carefully plan routes, and never compromise on safety standards regardless of conditions.

    What Makes Us Different

    100% Local Team

    Unlike companies that hire guides from elsewhere, every member of our team is from the Sapa region. This means authentic local knowledge, genuine cultural insights, and a personal connection to the places we visit. Our guides aren't reading from scripts—they're sharing their home.

    Small Group Commitment

    We limit all treks to maximum 8 people to ensure personalized attention, minimize environmental impact, and maintain the intimate, authentic feel that makes our experiences special. You'll never feel like just another tourist in a crowd.

    Flexible & Customizable

    While we offer set itineraries, we're happy to customize treks to match your interests, fitness level, and time constraints. Want to focus on photography? Interested in traditional textiles? Need a gentler pace? We'll adapt to create your ideal experience.

    Beyond the Tourist Trail

    Our google photo gallery showcases locations you won't find in standard guidebooks. We know the hidden waterfalls, secret viewpoints, and lesser-visited villages that offer more authentic experiences away from tourist crowds.
